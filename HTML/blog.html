Low and Slow

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking techniques called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, yu submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In this book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching
