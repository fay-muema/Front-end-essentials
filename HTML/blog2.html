Low and Slow

posted by BGB , November 15, 2012

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic ) into water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature of the food. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff potter describes it as ultra-low-temparature poaching
